# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in City Pop respectful and harassment-free, regardless of experience, identity, background, ability, or technical preference.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing technical and safety concerns directly.
- Respect privacy, authorization boundaries, and applicable law.
- Do not post credentials, private targets, captured personal data, or exploit details that create an immediate risk.
- Accept maintainer decisions about safety, scope, and project direction.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, deliberate disruption, unauthorized testing, and publishing another person’s sensitive information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens people, the community, or the project. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner’s GitHub profile or an appropriate private repository channel. Do not disclose sensitive evidence in a public issue.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
